About Our Villa

Wild Heaven Beach Villa was established with a single vision: to provide a sanctuary where the rhythm of the ocean meets the comfort of home. Located in the heart of Unawatuna, we offer a unique blend of traditional Sri Lankan hospitality and modern luxury.

Our villa features spacious rooms with panoramic ocean views, a dedicated staff ready to cater to your every need, and a location that places you minutes away from the historic Galle Fort and the vibrant Unawatuna beach scene.

Our Location

We are situated in a quiet neighborhood, ensuring peace and privacy, yet just a short walk from the main beach road.
